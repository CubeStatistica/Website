About Us

The principal consultant of Cube Statistica is Faizan Khalid Mohsin. Faizan has been the principal consultant of Cube Statistica since 2016 when he founded the company. He has been the lead statistician for several studies and has also worked as either a data scientist or digital solutions consultant for several companies and institutions including:

Toronto General Hospital
The Hospital for Sick Children (SickKids)
Princess Margaret Cancer Centre
Advantage Group International
University of Toronto
Department of Finance Canada
